Edward Elric

Main Character of Animed Called Fullmetal Alchemist. Edward Elric makes a fatal mistake with his brother, Alphonse at a very young age by attempting Human Transmutation. Edward loses his right arm and left leg while his brother Alphonse loses his body. Determined to reverse the effects, he becomes the youngest State Alchemist to search for the legendary Philosopher's Stone.
